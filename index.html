About placement

Intake magnets sit on the upper sidewall of each nostril, where the nose flares from the bridge — the nasal valve area. The strips pull outward to widen this passage and open your airway.

Not affiliated with Intake Breathing. This is a placement guide, not a medical device — always follow the manufacturer's official instructions. All video stays on your device; nothing is uploaded.

Welcome 👋

This tool uses your webcam and on-device face tracking to show you exactly where to place your Intake breathing magnets.

Two glowing targets will appear on your nose. Place one Intake strip at each marker.

How this works

1. Allow camera access.

2. Position your face — look straight at the camera, fill ~half the frame.

3. Two green markers appear on the sides of your nose. That's where each magnet goes.

4. Place one Intake strip at each marker, pressing gently for ~5 seconds.

5. Take a snapshot to verify placement against the targets.

The app uses MediaPipe Face Landmarker (478 landmarks) to find the alar cartilage — the curve of your nose just above each nostril where the nasal valve sits.
